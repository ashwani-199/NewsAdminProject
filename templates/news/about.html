About {{ site_settings.site_name|default:"Bhartiya Janta News" }}

Delivering Truth. Empowering Citizens.

Welcome to {{ site_settings.site_name|default:"Bhartiya Janta News" }} — a trusted name in Indian journalism, committed to delivering accurate, timely, and impartial news to millions of readers across the nation and beyond.

Our Story

{{ site_settings.site_name|default:"Bhartiya Janta News" }} was founded with a singular vision — to create a news platform that puts the people first. In an era of misinformation and sensationalism, we believe that responsible journalism is the cornerstone of a healthy democracy. Since our inception, we have worked tirelessly to build a newsroom that values truth over clicks and substance over sensation.

Our Mission

To inform, educate, and empower every citizen of India with reliable and unbiased news coverage. We strive to give a voice to the voiceless, hold those in power accountable, and shine a light on the stories that matter most to the people of India.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and people-centric news platform — one that serves as a bridge between the government and the governed, fostering informed dialogue and strengthening the democratic fabric of our nation.

What We Cover

We provide comprehensive coverage across a wide range of topics that matter to everyday Indians:

  • National News — Political developments, government policies, parliamentary proceedings, and civic issues from every corner of India
  • State & Local News — In-depth reporting from states, districts, and villages that mainstream media often overlooks
  • World News — International affairs, diplomatic relations, and global events with an Indian perspective
  • Business & Economy — Market updates, industry news, startup stories, and economic policies that impact your livelihood
  • Technology — Digital India initiatives, startup ecosystem, gadgets, and the evolving tech landscape
  • Sports — Cricket, kabaddi, football, Olympics, and all Indian sports achievements
  • Entertainment — Bollywood, regional cinema, OTT platforms, and cultural events
  • Education — Board results, competitive exams, policy changes, and opportunities for students
  • Health — Public health updates, medical research, wellness tips, and healthcare accessibility
  • Agriculture & Rural — Farming policies, rural development, mandi prices, and stories from India's heartland

Our Editorial Values

  • Accuracy — Every story is thoroughly fact-checked before publication. We correct errors promptly and transparently.
  • Fairness — We present all sides of a story without bias, allowing readers to form their own opinions.
  • Independence — Our editorial decisions are free from political, corporate, or commercial influence.
  • Accountability — We take responsibility for our journalism and welcome scrutiny from our readers.
  • Empathy — We report with sensitivity, respecting the dignity and privacy of individuals affected by events.
